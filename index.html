// About the Lab

Alethia is an independent open intelligence laboratory. We apply rigorous analytical methodology to the study of artificial intelligence systems — their architecture, training regimes, and societal implications.

Our work is grounded in the conviction that transparency is prerequisite to accountability. All findings are published openly, contributing to a shared substrate of machine intelligence knowledge accessible to researchers worldwide.

"Aletheia — the ancient concept of disclosure, of things revealed from concealment. It is the appropriate name for work that insists on seeing clearly."
